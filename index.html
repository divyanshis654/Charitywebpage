NANHI UDAAN

WHO WE ARE AND WHAT DO WE DO?

Nanhi Udaan, a beacon of hope, is dedicated to transforming the lives of underprivileged and orphaned children. Through a tapestry of initiatives, including education drives, donation campaigns, teaching endeavors, and fostering adoption, we tirelessly work to provide these little souls with the wings they need to soar. At Nanhi Udaan, every child's dream is precious, and we are committed to lighting up their path to a brighter, more promising future.

WE TEACH THEM THE RIGHT WAY

Nanhi Udaan is a joint programme with the mission to empower and support underprivileged chidren by ensuring that every child has at least 1 adult assigned to them.

Donation drives

One man's trash is another man's treasure. Our needy children get donations every month from the privileged section. You never know, a pen to you, is worth a career to another.

Teaching

In todays world, everyone needs to be educated. To survive in this world, More literacy isn't enough. Our kids are taught alphabets to alpha beta gama. We make sure that at the end of the day, the child doesn't only have fun but learn somthing of value.

Adoption

Our mission is to ensure that every child, regardless of their background, finds a loving, stable, and permanent home where they can thrive and fulfill their potential. We are committed to upholding the highest ethical standards and child welfare principles in the adoption process, putting the best interests of the child at the forefront of our work.

Our Gallery

We reach out to children and make them interact with young people from universities and teach them in a friendly way so as to provide them with a family environment for learning, make them understand our culture and hardships they need to overcome in the journey of their life.

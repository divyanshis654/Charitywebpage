OUR PROJECT

Nanhi Udaan's projects are the heart of our mission. Each initiative is carefully designed to ignite the flames of hope and potential within underprivileged children. From educational drives and healthcare programs to creative workshops and mentorship endeavors, our projects are the building blocks of transformation. With unwavering commitment, we craft each project to uplift, nurture, and empower the young hearts we serve. Explore our diverse range of projects and witness the positive change they bring to the lives of these deserving children. With a team of dedicated educators, volunteers, and partners, we work tirelessly to break the cycle of poverty and empower these young minds to dream beyond their circumstances. We do that through our initiatives like :
Balakalpa (बालकल्प): An artistic and creative program that encourages children to explore their imagination through activities like drawing, painting, and craftwork.
Vidyarasa (विद्यारस): An educational initiative that aims to cultivate the love for learning among children by making the study of subjects engaging and enjoyable.
Ganitagana (गणितगण): A mathematics enrichment program designed to make math fun and accessible to children through games and interactive activities.
Vayovriddhi (वयोवृद्धि): A program that focuses on the holistic development of children, including physical fitness, nutrition, and overall well-being.
